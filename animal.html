Animal Libraly

Bear

Bears have large bodies with strong limbs, and their fur varies greatly in color and length depending on the species and habitat. They possess sharp claws and strong jaws. Most bear species have an acute sense of smell and are excellent climbers and swimmers.

Learn more

Raccoon

Raccoons have a robust body with greyish-brown fur and a distinctive black mask over their eyes. They have five toes on each paw and are highly dexterous, capable of manipulating objects with their front paws. They are also known for their adaptability to various environments.

Learn more

Beaver

Beavers have large, flat tails and powerful incisors adapted for gnawing wood. Their bodies are well-suited for aquatic life, with webbed hind feet and a waterproof coat. They can remain submerged for up to 15 minutes and have transparent eyelids to see underwater.

Learn more

Wolf

Wolves are large canids with strong jaws, long legs, and dense fur that varies in color from gray to white, brown, and black. They have keen senses of smell and hearing, and their social structures are highly organized, with packs typically consisting of a dominant breeding pair and their offspring.
