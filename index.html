About Us

Devoted to an educational and fun experience for all, Lelem Natural Park is home to many local and rescued animals. With our exhibits designed to replicate each animals natural habitats, we ensure a safe and caring environment for our animals, as well as providing our guests with an immersive experience.

Featured Animals

Beaver

Beavers are semi-aquatic rodents that have large teeth strong enough to gnaw through wood. They excel at underwater travel, with swim speeds up to 8km per hour. They assist in helping ecosystems and themselves, by creating dams. By creating dams, beavers help control the flow of water and control floods.

Learn more

Squirrel

Squirrels are small rodents that often live in a wide range of habitat from forests to urban areas. They're small, agile and have sharp fangs. They use their sharp fangs and claws to forage food like nuts, fruits, seeds and more. They bury food in order to use them during the Winter times.

Learn more

Bald Eagle

The bald eagle is known for their distinctive white head in contrast to their brown body. With a wide wingspan of 8 feet and huge stature, bald eagles are an impressive animal and bird of prey.
