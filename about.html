About Lelam Natural Park

Welcome to Lelam Natural Park, where wildlife conservation meets family fun! Nestled in the heart of Vancouver, our zoo is home to over 200 species from around the world, providing a sanctuary for endangered animals and a hub of educational experiences for visitors of all ages.

Our mission is to inspire and educate our community about the wonders of the natural world while actively contributing to the conservation of wildlife and their habitats. We are committed to creating an engaging and educational experience that fosters a deeper understanding and appreciation of wildlife and environmental stewardship.


At Lelam, we prioritize the well-being of our animals, offering state-of-the-art facilities and enrichment programs to ensure their health and happiness. Our dedicated team of caretakers, veterinarians, and volunteers work tirelessly to provide the best possible care for our animal residents. We offer a variety of programs and activities, from guided tours and animal encounters to educational workshops and conservation initiatives. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned member, there's always something new to discover at Lelam.


Join us in our mission to protect and preserve the incredible diversity of life on Earth. Together, we can make a difference for wildlife and the environment.
