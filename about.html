About Lelem Natural Park

Lelem Natural Park is committed to providing a safe sanctuary for animals across the globe. With over 100 rescued and local animals, we make an incredible effort to provide our animals with utmost care and love. Each exhibit is designed with each animal and species in mind, carefully reproducing their natural habitat so they feel right at home.

Our mission is to educate those around us about our animals. From school field trips, corporate events, or casual leisure. Lelem Natural Park is committed to educating about the conservation of wildlife, safety of animals, how animals interact with us and their special characteristics, and more. With guided tours, workshops, and fun events catered to all ages, there is always something to learn and do at Lelem.
